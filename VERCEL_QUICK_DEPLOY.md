## ✅ How to Add Variables

For each variable above:

1. **Copy the Name** (e.g., `NEXT_PUBLIC_FIREBASE_API_KEY`)
2. **Paste in "Name" field** on Vercel
3. **Copy the Value** (e.g., `AIzaSyBk0a0oUcdiIP2TeN9n5Qp2orXhAuSPH4c`)
4. **Paste in "Value" field** on Vercel
5. **Select "All"** for environments
6. **Click "Add"**
7. **Repeat for all 6 variables**

## 🔗 After Deployment

### 1. Get Your URL
You'll see: `https://eventra-[random].vercel.app`

### 2. Test Your App
Visit the URL and check:
- ✅ Home page loads
- ✅ Explore page works
- ✅ Login works

### 3. Seed Database
Visit: `https://your-url.vercel.app/admin/seed`
- Click "Seed Database"
- Wait for success

### 4. Update Firebase
1. Go to: https://console.firebase.google.com
2. Select: `eventra-5443d`
3. Go to: Authentication > Settings
4. Add domain: `your-vercel-url.vercel.app`
